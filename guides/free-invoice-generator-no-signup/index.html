What belongs on an invoice

If you're invoicing for the first time, make sure these are on it — clients' accounting departments often reject invoices missing one of them:

  • The word "Invoice" and a unique invoice number (e.g. INV-2026-014)
  • Your business name and contact details, and your client's
  • Issue date and due date (or payment terms like "Net 30")
  • Line items with quantity and unit price, plus subtotal, tax, and total due
  • How to pay you — bank details, PayPal, or whatever you accept, in the notes field

Invoice Studio has fields for all of these, so the structure is handled for you.
